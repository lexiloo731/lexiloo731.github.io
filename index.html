Bagel Angel

Game Designer / Programmer
Itch.io | Engine: Unity

Bagel Angel is a whimsical first-person shooter where players take control of a winged bagel angel navigating a giant bagel battlefield. Using a double-barreled shotgun and a butter knife sword, players face spread-themed enemies such as butter, jam, and honey, attempting to cover as much of the bagel surface as possible before defeat. The game combines fast-paced combat, strategic positioning, and creative enemy design for a unique and humorous experience.

  • Designed and implemented enemy AI and behaviors for different enemies.
  • Developed the scoring system to track player progress and bagel coverage.
  • Implemented UI elements for health, ammo, and score feedback to enhance player awareness.
  • Created 2D tiles in a 3D space for level design and enemy/item placement under a 2-week game jam deadline.
